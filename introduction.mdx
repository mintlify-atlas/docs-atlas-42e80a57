# Welcome to Meshery

Meshery is a self-service engineering platform and the open source, cloud native manager that enables the design and management of all Kubernetes-based infrastructure and applications. As a CNCF project, Meshery offers visual and collaborative GitOps, freeing you from the chains of YAML while managing Kubernetes multi-cluster deployments.

## What is Meshery?

Meshery provides a single pane of glass to manage multiple Kubernetes clusters across any infrastructure, including various cloud providers. It enables consistent configuration, operation, and observability across your entire Kubernetes landscape.

### Infrastructure Lifecycle Management

Meshery manages the configuration, deployment, and operation of your cloud services and Kubernetes clusters while supporting hundreds of different types of cloud native infrastructure integrations.

### Workspaces: Your Team's Collaboration Hub

<Note>
Workspaces serve as the central point of collaboration for you and your teams, providing organized access control to environments and their resources.
</Note>

Workspaces let you organize your work similarly to Google Drive for cloud native projects. They make it easier to manage, share, and work with a collection of resources as a group.

### Performance Management

Meshery offers load generation and performance characterization to help you assess and optimize the performance of your applications and infrastructure.

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Load Testing" icon="gauge-high">

  <Card title="SMP Specification" icon="file-contract">
    Standards-based performance measurement using Cloud Native Performance spec
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Platform Engineering with Extensibility

<Info>
Extend Meshery as your self-service engineering platform using gRPC adapters, hot-loadable React packages, Golang plugins, and REST/GraphQL APIs.
</Info>

The extensive set of extension points in Meshery make it ideal as the foundation of your internal developer platform:

- **Adapters**: gRPC services for service mesh and infrastructure integrations
- **Providers**: Authentication and preference management plugins
- **UI Extensions**: Hot-loadable React components and provider-specific interfaces
- **GraphQL Subscriptions**: Real-time event streaming and state updates
